The Developer

My passion lies with learning and improving the way people communicate with and through technology. Even though I am a full stack developer, I tend to gravite more towards the front-end side. Working on the client-side, I am able to influence what the user sees and how they interact with a platform. Plus, I really enjoy working and collaborating on an apps design.

While studying at the University of Illinois, I developed an web application (FimR) designed to connect creative students. I created FimR to help students at U of I find other students that shared the same passions and help manage their personal projects more effectively. Unfortunately, FimR went offline in 2018. I learned a lot from this experience that I plan on applying to new apps.

My ultimate goal is to create a successful startup that further my passion of improving the way people communicate.

The Filmmaker

I am a filmmaker hobbyist. In my spare time, I create short films spanning different genres. I have helped write, direct, and act in several student short films while at the University of Illinois.

I am a creator at heart, whether it be film or web apps.
